Mexican Beef Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning mix
  • 1/2 cup onion, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • Sour cream for topping
  • Guacamole for topping

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.
  2. Stir in the taco seasoning, diced tomatoes, and salsa. Cook and stir for 5 minutes or until heated through.
  3. Warm taco shells as per package instructions.
  4. To assemble tacos, fill each shell with the beef mixture
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
