Italian Beef Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling salted water for 10 minutes, or until al dente. Drain.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, 1/2 cup Parmesan, basil, and egg.
  6. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
