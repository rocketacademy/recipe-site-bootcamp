Kyushu Chicken Ramen

Ingredients

Chicken
Noodle
Soup

Time

10 Minutes

Instructions

Bring 2 1/2 cups of water to a boil in a small saucepan.

Add the noodles and cook for 2 minutes.

Add the flavor packet, stir, and continue to cook for another 30 seconds.

Remove the pan from the heat and carefully add the egg.

Carefully transfer everything to a serving bowl, add the butter, cheese and sesame seeds and mix.
