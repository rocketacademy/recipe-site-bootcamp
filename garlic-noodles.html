Garlic and Spring Onion Noodles

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce Noodles/li>
  • 8 Garlic Cloves
  • 1/2 Cup Spring Onions chopped
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Seeds
  • 1 tbsp Black Pepper
  • Salt to taste

Noodle Sauce

  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Ketchup
  • 2 tbsp Chilli Sauce/Sriracha
